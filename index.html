About Me

Born in Baltimore Md, I graduated from Morgan State University with my degree in Information Sciences & Systems. With this degree, I was able to be exposed to a lot of different sides to the IT industry. After graduating I decided to start a new in California. I moved at the end of the year 2016 and my life began in California in 2017. Currently, I am a Desktop Technician at Mission Produce Inc. Here at Mission, I was able to grow in technical knowledge as well as personal knowledge as I applied what I learned on the job to things outside of my job. At this company, I have been recognized for my continued hard work and always going above and beyond for end users.


Now I am seeking a career change into a junior web development role to be able to help create but also learn how to create more. I always had a curiosity and an attraction to the web developer world but it was not until recently that I decided I would take the leap to transition into this career. I consider my greatest strength to be my hardworking attitude and my willingness to learn. This has brought me to take classes such as the Web Development Bootcamp as well as classes at Skillcrush Academy. Combined these classes have covered everything from front end to back end development which I believe would help me with my career transitioning.
